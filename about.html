Our Origin Story

In 2010, Dr. Kessler, Dr. Becchetti, and Dr. Karimov were the first to bring sophisticated Functional Evaluation Technology — including Heart Rate Variability — from Russia to the United States. They spent years translating the research, developing manuals, and teaching the technology to clinicians across North America.

After teaching this technology for over 10 years, the team made the decision to break away from the Russian platform and develop an entirely new, improved system — one built from the ground up with their own clinical wisdom, a global team of experts, and modern software architecture. The result is CRIS GOLD™ — the most advanced clinical HRV and Eastern medicine assessment platform available today.
