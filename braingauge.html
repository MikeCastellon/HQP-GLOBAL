What Is BrainGauge

Objective Brain Health Metrics You Can Track

The BrainGauge, developed by Dr. Mark Tommerdahl and Cortical Metrics, is a neuroscience-validated device that measures cortical function through somatosensory testing. When integrated with CRIS GOLD™, it provides a comprehensive picture of brain health alongside autonomic nervous system function.

Rather than subjective cognitive questionnaires, the BrainGauge uses precise vibrotactile stimulation to measure how your brain processes sensory information — providing quantifiable, reproducible neurological metrics.

🧠

Cortical Metrics Assessment

Measures how well your patient's brain distinguishes between different sensory inputs (amplitude discrimination), processes the timing and order of events (temporal order judgment), and differentiates subtle signal changes (frequency discrimination). These reveal whether the brain is processing information accurately — critical for identifying neurological issues that subjective questionnaires miss.

⚡

Brain Speed & Processing

Measures how fast your patient's brain responds to stimuli — a key indicator of overall brain health. When processing speed declines, patients experience brain fog, slower reactions, and difficulty concentrating. This metric helps you objectively track improvement or decline from concussion, aging, chronic illness, or treatment interventions.

📊

Reproducible Tracking

Every test produces a quantifiable score you can compare visit to visit. Show patients objective proof that their brain function is improving — or catch early decline before symptoms worsen. Ideal for pre/post concussion baselines and measuring treatment outcomes.

🏋️

Brain Training Exercises

Dr. Kessler has developed a series of brain training exercises that can be used alongside BrainGauge measurement to improve cortical metrics over time.
