Additional Features

Built-in Clinical Questionnaires

CRIS GOLD™ includes embedded clinical questionnaires that complement HRV data — providing a more comprehensive picture of patient health and treatment response.

01

Stress Questionnaire

A validated stress assessment tool embedded directly in the CRIS GOLD™ interface. Correlates patient-reported stress with objective HRV stress markers for a complete picture of sympathetic burden.

  • Perceived stress scale
  • Lifestyle stressor inventory
  • HRV correlation overlay
  • Trend tracking over visits
02

Nutritional / Metabolic Questionnaire

A comprehensive metabolic questionnaire that identifies nutritional deficiencies and imbalances affecting autonomic function. Cross-referenced with HRV metabolic markers in the CRIS GOLD™ analysis.

  • Dietary pattern assessment
  • Nutrient deficiency screening
  • Metabolic health indicators
  • Supplement recommendation support
03

Neurotransmitter Questionnaire

Assessment of neurotransmitter imbalances — serotonin, dopamine, GABA, and epinephrine — based on symptom patterns. Provides context for HRV findings related to brain-heart axis function.

  • Serotonin deficit screening
  • Dopamine pathway assessment
  • GABA/anxiety correlation
  • Treatment protocol guidance
