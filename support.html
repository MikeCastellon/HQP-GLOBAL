1

Opening the Contents

Unbox your CRIS GOLD™ device and identify all components — the sensor, Bluetooth adapter, cables, and accessories. Verify everything is present before proceeding.

2

Bluetooth & Software Setup

FAQ

Common Questions

What operating systems does CRIS GOLD™ support?
CRIS GOLD™ software is compatible with Windows 10/11 and macOS. For the latest system requirements, please contact our support team or check the documentation provided with your device.
How long does a typical patient session take?
A standard CRIS GOLD™ assessment takes approximately 5 minutes — including sensor attachment, ECG capture, and report generation. Some users complete it in as little as 3–4 minutes once they're comfortable with the workflow.
Can I use CRIS GOLD™ in my home instead of a clinic?
Yes — CRIS GOLD™ is designed for both clinical and home use. The device is compact and portable, and the software runs on standard Windows or macOS computers.
Are software updates included with my purchase?
Yes — all CRIS GOLD™ software updates are included for the lifetime of your device. New features, modules, and parameter improvements are delivered automatically as they are released.
What is your return and warranty policy?
Please contact us directly at heartquestglobal@gmail.com or +1 (415) 646-6112 to discuss our warranty and return terms. We stand behind our product and will work with you to resolve any issues.
Do you offer training for my staff?
Yes — every CRIS GOLD™ purchase includes a personal onboarding session and access to educational resources. We also offer expanded training and can arrange additional sessions for larger practices.
