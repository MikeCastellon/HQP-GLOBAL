How It Works

🔬 Clinical Precision. Simple Setup.
Reproducible Results.

The RJL Quantum VII uses bioelectrical impedance analysis — a safe, validated, non-invasive method that delivers four key clinical measurements every time, with no guesswork and no radiation.

⚡

Bioelectrical Impedance

A safe, low-level electrical signal passes through the body. Resistance and reactance measurements reveal the precise composition of lean tissue, fat, and water at the cellular level.

📐

Phase Angle

The phase angle — derived from resistance and reactance — is a direct marker of cell membrane integrity and cellular health. Low phase angle signals disease risk and malnutrition.

💧

Hydration & Water Balance

ECW/ICW ratios reveal inflammation, edema, and cellular hydration status — critical markers often invisible to standard assessments.

🔥

Metabolic Rate Calculation

Resting metabolic rate derived from lean mass — giving you accurate caloric targets for weight management, recovery, and performance protocols.
